About the Artist

Hello! I’m Anca, an artist with a fascination for the dark and surreal. My work is inspired by the unknown and the eerie beauty hidden within shadows. At the moment, I’m working on a series called **The Daily Sculptures**, where I create one small sculpture every day. These figurines are crafted to capture the haunting beauty of the forgotten and the otherworldly. I sell my work through a waiting list. When you’re on it, you will receive an email every two months with the available sculptures. Join here to get access and be notified when the next series is released.
