About the Artist

Hello! I’m Anca, an artist with a fascination for the dark and surreal. My work is inspired by the unknown and the eerie beauty hidden within shadows. At the moment, I’m working on a series called **The Daily Sculptures**, where I create one small sculpture every day. These figurines are crafted to capture the macabre essence of otherworldly beings, forgotten realms, and surreal dreams.
